Funnel Blueprint: Natty Kay / Operation Hair

Prepared by: Andrei Givero, AG Consulting Date: May 17, 2026

Funnel Overview

The current funnel is: IG post or DM -> Booksy -> appointment. That is a two-step path with no email capture, no nurture, no upsell, and no retention. Every new client starts from scratch, and every lost client stays lost.

The proposed funnel adds an email layer, a conversion-optimized landing page, an in-salon upsell, and a post-appointment sequence that turns one-time clients into repeat clients and referrers.
