  • Face cam on. Background does not need to be perfect. Authentic beats polished here.
  • Do not read this script word for word. Use it as a talk track. Her message was casual; your video should match.

Talk Track (3 Minutes)

No intro, no pleasantries, no agenda slide. Jump straight in.
