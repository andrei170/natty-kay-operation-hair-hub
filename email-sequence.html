Phase 1+2

Email Sequence: Operation Hair New Client Welcome Series

Prepared by: Andrei Givero, AG Consulting Date: May 17, 2026 Trigger: New client email captured (post-booking or via lead magnet opt-in) Sequence length: 5 emails over 90 days Sender name: Natalie from Operation Hair

Voice Notes

These emails are from Natalie, not from a marketing department. The voice is direct, warm, and specific to the service the client just received. No corporate language. No triple-adjective lists. No "we are thrilled to have you." Write like Natalie would text a friend who just left the chair.

Email 1: Day 1 (Appointment Day or Next Morning)

Subject: Your hair looks good. Here is how to keep it that way.

Body:

Hi [First Name],

Really glad you came in today.

Here is what to do in the next 48 hours to protect your [SERVICE TYPE: braids / extensions / install / color]:

  • [SERVICE-SPECIFIC TIP 1 -- e.g., for extensions: "Do not wash for the first 48 hours. Let the bonds settle."]
  • [SERVICE-SPECIFIC TIP 2 -- e.g., "Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase if you have one. It makes a real difference."]
  • [SERVICE-SPECIFIC TIP 3 -- e.g., "If anything feels uncomfortable around the hairline in the first 24 hours, that is normal. If it does not ease up by day 3, message me."]

If you have questions, just reply to this email.

See you next time.

  • Natalie

Email 2: Day 7

Subject: One week check-in.

Body:

Hi [First Name],

It has been a week since you were in. How are you feeling about your [braids / extensions / color]?

One thing I see clients skip and then regret:

[SERVICE-SPECIFIC MAINTENANCE TIP -- e.g., for braids: "Spray your scalp with a light oil (I use tea tree + water mix) every 2-3 days. It keeps the scalp from drying out and makes your braids last significantly longer."]

You can grab what I recommend here: [link to product or shop page -- SWAP-SLOT, confirm product links with Natalie at kickoff]

If you want the full guide I put together on [braid maintenance / extension care / color upkeep], reply "GUIDE" and I will send it over.

  • Natalie

Email 3: Day 30-45

Subject: Time to refresh?

Body:

Hi [First Name],

It has been about [4 to 6 weeks] since your appointment. Depending on what we did, you are probably at the point where a touch-up or rebook makes sense.

[SERVICE-SPECIFIC REBOOK PROMPT -- e.g., for extensions: "Tape-ins typically need a move-up around 6-8 weeks. If you wait much longer, the bonds start to tangle."]

I have some availability this week and next. Book here: [Booksy link]

If you want to try something different this time, just reply and tell me what you are thinking. I can let you know if it is doable.

  • Natalie

Email 4: Day 60

Subject: Quick ask.

Body:

Hi [First Name],

Hope everything is going well.

I want to ask you something directly: if you know anyone who has been looking for a good hairstylist in the Bay Area, would you be willing to send them my way?

You do not need to do anything elaborate. Just forward this email or share my IG (@nattykay7) with them.

If they book and mention your name, I will take $[SWAP-SLOT -- confirm referral amount with Natalie] off your next appointment.

That is it. No complicated referral program. Just a name.

  • Natalie

Email 5: Day 90 (Re-engagement)

Subject: Haven't seen you in a while.

Body:

Hi [First Name],

It has been about three months since you were in. Checking in.

If life got busy, no worries. Happens to everyone.

If you have been meaning to rebook but have not gotten around to it, here is the link: [Booksy link]

If something about the last visit was not right, I would actually want to know. You can reply directly.

Either way, the chair is here when you are ready.

  • Natalie, Operation Hair

Notes for Kickoff

  • Confirm email platform (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or ConvertKit recommended)
  • Replace all [SWAP-SLOT] fields with Natalie's confirmed details before launch
  • Service-specific email variants: create separate versions for extensions, braids, color, and wig installs so the tips are specific to what each client received
  • Referral discount amount: confirm with Natalie (suggest $25 or 10% off next service)
  • Product links in Email 2: confirm what products Natalie recommends and stocks; do not link to Amazon generics
  • Sender name should show as "Natalie from Operation Hair" not "noreply@operationhair.com"
